Optimised Game Settings

2025-05-27

warframeqolsettings

Gameplay

Reload with Context Action Input (OFF)

Melee Auto Targetting (OFF, doesnt matter too much though as its that… intrusive)

Align Attacks to Camera: (OFF) (no longer yanks your melee to your camera so you have to swing your camera around to turn where you aim, also makes the game’s melee feel so much nicer as you slam down without needing to be schizophrenic)

System:

Legacy Colour Pallates: OFF (default) (Note: swapping back and forth will nuke your favourited colours curation)

Network:

Region: N/A or Asia (any works but these are most popular, I find people even in australia)

Matchmaking Ping Limit (up to you, 300 is default)

Enable cross platform play: ON

Social:

Chat:

  • Region Chat (off)

  • Q&A (on)

  • Recruiting (on)

  • Trade (OFF OFF OFF OFF OFF OFF OFF OFF OFF)

  • Show chat mesage timestamps (on)

  • inline private messages (OFF) (ON by default) (OFF, means that whispers will be kept to the same person, they dont interrupt others)

  • enable profanity filter (off) (Optional)

Chat Appearance:

  • :) -> :SMILE: Conversion (OFF) that way we can do smiley faces

Interface:

Adjust HUD Margins (Preference)

Show damage numbers (Preference) (Legacy is better imo, it doesnt ‘stain’ or stay on your screen) Compact Damage numbers (Preference (I have it off, I wanna see big number, not think about if its big number)

Show health over enemies, (Preference), (ON , turning it off does bring it back to older times)

Video:

Display

Vsync: OFF

Brightness: 50 -> 32 (stay between 32-50)

Contrast: 50

Field of View: 65 -> 90 (Max)

Upscaling:

OFF.

Graphics:

  • Anti-Aliasing (OFF) (Preference, just don’t use TAA)

Advanced:

  • Motion Blur (OFF)

  • Depth of Field (OFF)

  • Distoritons (OFF)

  • Glare (OFF)

  • Film Grain (OFF)

  • Bloom: 50 -> 15 / OFF

  • Character Shadows (ON)

  • Reduced Frame Latency (ON) (sound good, I personally haven’t actually noticed an effect)

Accessibility

Keyboard/Mouse:

Fire Manual Trigger Weapons Continously: ON

Video:

Visual Effects Intensity: 100%, dependant Note: Direct input lets you hard-set this to 0.

Turn off Enable Screen Shake (please)

Character Highlighting:

Enemy Highlights: (Preference) (ON, 50% - I personally like the visual clarity)

Reduce Teammates Visual Effects (ON)
